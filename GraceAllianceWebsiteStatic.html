At Grace Alliance Church, we believe that the Gospel changes everything. We seek to become a genuine community in which you can experience the reality of that Gospel. We invite you to come check us out. We’d love to meet you in person, hear your story, pray for you, and cheer you on with the greatest story of all, the Gospel of Jesus Christ.



We are a community of inadequate but transformed people. We are inadequate because of our shortcomings, but transformed by the grace of God. We desire to grow in grace and we seek to extend that grace to our neighbors in the Santa Clara Valley.

As a diverse Vietnamese American church community since 1983, we recognize our unique blessings, able to draw from the faithful heritage of our 1st-generation and benefit from the innovation and energy of the younger generations. And thus we embrace diversity, we use both Vietnamese and English, we practice grace and acceptance, and we obey God to travel an unknown road toward grace expansion.
